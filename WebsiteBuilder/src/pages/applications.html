You can apply to do various things at and for Pawske Party, here is an overview.
DJ Applications
Do you wanna help us make Pawske Party the ultimate furry party? We need DJ’s to share their skills with us and get the dancefloor bouncing. Experienced or not, you might be just the person we need to make a night of varied music styles and fun for all. Apply through the Google form below. Last chance to apply is February 1st, but the form might close earlier if we get enough applications to fill the available time slots.
All DJ’s get free entrance, a free t-shirt, and a free drink.

Sign-up form click here
Dealers Den Applications
Want to sell something at Pawske Party? We have a limited number of spots available for people who want to deal.
Apply by emailing us at pawskeparty@gmail.com and tell us a bit about what you will be selling. Deadline February 1st.
Event Applications
Pawske Party is more than just a dance, we strive to provide a diverse daytime programme before we let the DJ’s loose. Do you wish to host an event? Fill out the form below and we will get back to you.
Click here
Staff Applications
We currently need one new security member to help make sure Pawske Party is a safe and comfortable event. The main objective of our security team is to be available if something happens, make sure everyone is well behaved, and crowd control.
Send an email to pawskeparty@gmail.com if you think this is something for you, or if you have questions about volunteering.
Artwork
We are always on the lookout for more artwork and art assets. If you want to help us with a specific project, or just send us something you have made, we would love to hear from you. Email us at pawskeparty@gmail.com
