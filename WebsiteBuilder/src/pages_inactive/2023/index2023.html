At Pawske Party you can do it all!
After several years of planning, and pandemic related delays, Pawske Party is finally here. Evolved from an annual Easter furwalk that grew too big for private homes, Norways first furdance is born.
We offer a full day of activities with panels, a small dealers den, and a furwalk. All culminating in an evening of dancing and DJ’s. Check out our practical info page for all you need to know.
Coming from afar? Our partner hotel Comfort Express Youngstorget offers all attendees 10% off their stay between April 5th and April 7th.
Why not make it a full blown Easter holiday? For early arrivals there will be a fursuit bowling on April 5th, and a relaxing spa day on April 7th. Read more about this on our Pre & Post Events page. Here you will also find tips on what else there is to see and do in Oslo.
Tickets go on sale Saturday February 11th at 15:00.
Make sure to check out of practical info page for more details, and important information.

Venue & Surroundings
Melahuset is a multicultural institution with special focus on diversity, and cultures that are well represented in Norway through immigration. It is located in the middle of Oslo, just a short walk from Jernbanetorget where the bus terminal and central station are located.
The surrounding area is dominated by bars and restaurants and is one of the city's major nightlife districts.
Read more at:
https://www.mela.no/melahuset/
