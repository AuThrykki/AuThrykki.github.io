Practical Info

General:

  • The age limit is 18 years.
  • Tickets can only be bought online.
  • Tickets are non refundable and non transferable.
  • Valid photo ID must be presented upon arrival.

Venue:

  • Melahuset is located at Mariboes gate 8, 0183 Oslo.
  • The doors will open at 10:30.
  • Opening ceremony at 11:30.
  • Last admissions at midnight.
  • Event ends at 01:30.
  • All guests must have left the venue by 01:50.
  • The bar is open from 19:00-01:00, between 10:30 and 18:00 you can consume your own non-alcoholic beverage.
  • We have limited storage space, please don’t bring more than you need to.

Ticket types:

  • Normal ticket
    • Costs 300 NOK
    • Available until midnight March 31st.
  • Sponsor ticket
    • Costs 550 NOK
    • Available until midnight on March 9th.
    • Includes an exclusive branded gym bag with some surprises (the more sponsor tickets we sell the better the surprises!), reserved front row seats on seated events, and our eternal gratitude.
  • Door ticket.
    • Costs 400 NOK
    • Available from April 1st until 23:45 on April 6th.
    • Can only be bought online, despite the name.
  • Upgrades:
    • Official t-shirt 250 NOK - available until midnight March 9th.
    • Meze buffet 285 NOK - available until 12:00 March 20th.
    • Pre-party bowling 180 NOK - available until midnight April 2nd.
    • Post-party spa 840 NOK - available until midnight March 23rd.

Hotel:

  • Our partner hotel is Comfort Hotel® Xpress Youngstorget.
  • Located only a few minutes walk from the venue.
  • All attendees get 10% off their stay with the discount code provided in the confirmation email.

Pre and post events:

  • Early arrival city walk.
    • Afternoon walk around downtown Oslo on April 5th to see some of what the city has to offer.
    • Free event, no ticket required.
    • Meet-up by the tiger statue at Jernbanetorget at noon.
    • Fursuits are welcome, but no fursuit support is provided.
    • The walk ends at Outland pop culture store at ca 15:00.
  • Fursuit bowling.
    • April 5th at Veitvet bowling from 17-19.
    • Changing area provided.
    • Ticket required, costs 180 kr.
    • Ticket includes up to 2 rounds of bowling per person.
    • Food and drink can be bought on site.
  • Post-Party Spa Day.
    • April 7th at The Well spa resort.
    • Meetup directly on location by 12:20.
    • Entrance to the resort at 12:30.
    • Late admissions at the discretion of the receptionists.
    • Costs 840 kr.
    • Includes lunch (pizza and salad). Served in Brasseriet at 15:00.
    • No phones, cameras or other electronics allowed beyond the locker rooms.
    • Nudity is allowed in all attractions, and required in most saunas.
    • Only swimwear bought on site is allowed in the resort.
    • Towels, bathrobe, slippers and water are included. You do not need to bring anything.
    • All guests must fill out the checkin [https://checkout.thewell.no/checkin] page and agree to the resort's rules before arrival.

Dinner - on the 6th

Food is important if you want the energy to party all night! We have reserved plenty of seats from 18:00 to 19:00 at Oslo Street Food, a food court located just two minutes walking from the venue. With a slew of interesting stalls offering flavours from all over the world everyone will find something to enjoy here.
